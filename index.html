Dr. Joseph Mercola

Considered one of the controversial and visionary alternative medical professionals. He has been an advocate of a great deal of eating lifestyles to improve both physical and emotional health.

Books

Dr. Mercola has written a great deal about the curative powers of a healthy diet. His recent book Fat for Fuel is about using a high fat low carb diet to help heal cancer. In addition to Fat for Fuel he has written…

  • Effortless Healing
  • Generation XL
  • The No-Grain Diet
  • Sweet Deception
  • And many more…

Since 1995 Dr. Mercola has had a website to better spread his message and help people understand the importance of what they are eating. For more information about him please visit Mercola.com
